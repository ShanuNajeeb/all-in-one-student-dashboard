How It Helps

Our dashboard is designed to empower students by streamlining their study routines and boosting productivity.

  • 📅 Organized Scheduling: Keep track of assignments and deadlines effortlessly.
  • ⏳ Focused Sessions: Use Pomodoro timers to enhance concentration and manage breaks.
  • 🃏 Effective Flashcards: Create and review flashcards to reinforce learning.
  • 🤖 AI Summarization: Quickly understand complex topics with AI-generated summaries.
  • 🎨 Custom Themes: Personalize your dashboard to suit your style and mood.
  • 🔒 Privacy First: No account needed, your data stays local and secure.
